⚠️ PoC: CSRF Checkout Trigger — alts.cool (F-093)

Finding: F-093 · Severity: HIGH
Target: POST https://alts.cool/api/payment/checkout
Root cause: Checkout endpoint processes cross-origin JSON POST; returns HTTP 400 (validation error) rather than HTTP 401 (unauthorized) on unauthenticated cross-origin requests.
Impact: An authenticated customer who visits a malicious page has their session relayed to trigger a purchase. The attacker chooses the product and quantity. The victim pays.
